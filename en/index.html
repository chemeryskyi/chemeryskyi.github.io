About us

Lobby X is a social enterprise of a unique format:platform + recruiting agency

  • We recruit workers for progressive businesses, reform teams, effective NGOs and independent media
  • A socially responsible platform for candidate search
  • We provide services to non-profit organizations free of charge
  • Working with Lobby X, you are driving the change in the country, investing in staffing reformation teams and community initiatives

OUR MISSION

  • We recruit for the progress of Ukraine
  • We are working to attract the Ukrainian diaspora to contribute to the "change sector" in Ukraine
  • We are building new communities in the agglomerations of the Ukrainian diaspora. Warsaw is our first city in this mission.
  • Human Capital is the most valuable capital on Earth. We strive to optimize and modernize the processes in the labour market
